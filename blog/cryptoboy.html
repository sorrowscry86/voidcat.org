What Makes CryptoBoy Special?

  • Real‑Time News AI: CryptoBoy scours top crypto news feeds, processing market sentiment with a local LLM (Ollama) before making any moves. It’s like having a tireless analyst at your side, 24/7.
  • Classic Meets Cutting‑Edge: Proven technical indicators — RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands — combined with real‑time AI insight for an edge few bots can match.
  • Risk Control Built In: From aggressive stop‑losses to trade caps and dry‑run backtesting, safety is treated as a core feature, not an afterthought.
  • Plug‑and‑Play Tech: Full Docker support, API hooks, Telegram alerts, and lucid docs make it accessible beyond hardcore coders.
