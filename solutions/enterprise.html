The Challenge

Enterprise teams need AI capabilities—but cloud services create data residency, compliance, and intellectual property risks:

  • Data leakage: Trade secrets and proprietary information sent to cloud AI services. No audit trail. GDPR/HIPAA violations risk.
  • Regulatory compliance: Healthcare (HIPAA), finance (PCI), legal (attorney-client privilege) require on-premise or secure data handling.
  • IP protection: Researchers, consultants, creatives cannot upload confidential work to external systems.
  • Model vendor lock-in: Reliant on single provider. Cost increases unpredictable. API changes break workflows.
  • Performance & latency: Network dependency adds unpredictable latency. Airgapped systems have zero options.
