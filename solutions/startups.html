The Challenge

Founders face critical funding gaps between seed and Series A. While VCs focus on growth-stage, grants provide non-dilutive capital for R&D. But finding and applying for grants is a bottleneck:

  • Discovery burden: 1,000+ grant sources across federal, state, and private agencies. Founders spend weeks researching.
  • Time drain: Proposal writing takes 40-80 hours per application. Opportunity cost is enormous.
  • Hit rate: Traditional approach has 15-25% success rate. Most proposals are rejected.
  • Compliance risk: Each grant has unique requirements (SBIR Phase 1 vs Phase 2, state-specific rules). Non-compliance = automatic rejection.
